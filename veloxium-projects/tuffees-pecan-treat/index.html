Our Signature Treat

Dive into the details of our most beloved classic: the perfect buttery, salty, crunchy pecan.

Classic Buttered Pecans

$18.95

This is where it all started. We take the finest Mammoth pecan halves from our Georgia grove, slow-roast them in small batches, and toss them in real creamery butter and a delicate dusting of sea salt while they're still warm. The result is a simple, perfect snack that's rich, savory, and incredibly moreish.

  • Ingredients: Georgia Pecans, Butter (Cream, Salt), Sea Salt.
  • Source: Sourced directly from Calloway Farms, Jasper County, GA.
  • Process: Hand-roasted in copper kettles.
  • Size: 8 oz. resealable bag to maintain freshness.

Our Story Is a Family Story

It all started with a tough-as-nails grandmother and her prized pecan trees.

From Tuffee's Kitchen to Yours

Our great-grandmother, Elouise Calloway, earned the nickname "Tuffee" for her unwavering spirit and her refusal to sell the family's pecan grove during hard times. For her, the trees were more than a crop; they were a legacy. Her kitchen was the heart of our family, where she transformed the yearly harvest into treats that marked every celebration and soothed every sorrow.

We founded Tuffee's Pecan Treats to honor her memory and share that same sense of warmth and tradition. We still harvest from those same trees, using her time-honored recipes as our guide. Every bag we ship is a piece of our history and a continuation of her love.
