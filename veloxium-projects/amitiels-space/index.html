Try This At Home: Fizzy Potions!

Create a colorful, bubbling reaction with simple kitchen ingredients. It's safe, fun, and super scientific!

  • You'll need: A clear glass or jar, baking soda, vinegar, and a few drops of food coloring.
  • Step 1: Put a few spoonfuls of baking soda at the bottom of the jar.
  • Step 2: Add a few drops of your favorite food coloring.
  • Step 3: Slowly pour in some vinegar and watch the fizzy magic happen! You just created a chemical reaction between an acid (vinegar) and a base (baking soda).
