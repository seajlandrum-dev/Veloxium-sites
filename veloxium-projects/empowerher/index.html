Our Mission

Building the Next Generation of Female Leaders

At EmpowerHer, we believe that true empowerment comes from a combination of physical confidence and intellectual prowess. Our unique model blends the strategic discipline of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with future-focused education in AI and technology.

We provide a safe, inclusive, and challenging environment where young women can discover their strength, sharpen their minds, and build a supportive community. We're not just teaching skills; we're building character, resilience, and the unshakeable belief that they can achieve anything.

Our Core Programs

We offer two distinct but complementary paths designed to build a holistic foundation for success.

The Mat: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Develop physical confidence, discipline, and problem-solving skills under pressure. Our BJJ program is designed for all levels, focusing on self-defense, body awareness, and the mental fortitude to overcome challenges.

Enroll in BJJ

The Code: AI & Technology

Unlock the future with our hands-on AI and coding workshops. We demystify technology, teaching fundamental programming concepts, ethical AI, and how to build projects that solve real-world problems.

Become a Catalyst for Change

Your time and expertise can create a lasting impact. By volunteering with EmpowerHer, you help shape the next generation of female leaders and innovators.

We are looking for passionate individuals to join our mission. Whether you're a BJJ black belt, a software engineer, or just someone who wants to help, there's a place for you here.

Current Opportunities:

  • AI & Coding Mentor: Guide students through projects and concepts.
  • BJJ Assistant Instructor: Help lead classes and ensure a safe training environment.
  • Event & Workshop Support: Assist with logistics for our community events.
  • Professional Mentor: Share your career journey and advice with our older participants.
