Your Questions, Answered!

How old do I have to be?

The Go-Go Gummy Club is perfect for kids who are 6 to 10 years old. If you love to play, create, and laugh, you'll fit right in!

Is it free to join?

Yes, totally and completely 100% free! All our games, activities, and fun are free for every Gummy Pal. No secret charges, ever!

What do we do in the club?

We do all sorts of awesome stuff! We play games in Red Cherry Racers, make cool stuff in Green Kiwi Creators, and tell jokes in Golden Lemon Laughs. Every week there's something new!

Can I invite my best friend?

Of course! The more Gummy Pals, the better! Show this website to your friend and have their grown-up help them sign up too. You can race and create together!
