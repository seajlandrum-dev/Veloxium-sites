About King Adonis

Intelligence, Compassion, and Discipline

King Adonis is not your average 8-year-old. With an insatiable curiosity for the world and a heart full of empathy, he embodies a new vision of what it means to be a young man. His days are a balance of rigorous learning, disciplined practice, and joyful play, all guided by a strong moral compass.

He believes that true strength lies not in being the loudest, but in being the most thoughtful, helpful, and resilient. From the chess board to the community garden, King applies his core values to every challenge he meets, aiming to leave every place and every person better than he found them.

Activities & Achievements

Tae Kwon Do - Green Belt

King practices Tae Kwon Do three times a week, focusing on forms, sparring, and the art's five tenets: Courtesy, Integrity, Perseverance, Self-Control, and Indomitable Spirit.

Junior Coder's Club

Using Scratch and introductory Python, King builds simple games and animations. His latest project is a maze game that teaches players about recycling.

Community Garden Volunteer

Every Saturday, King helps tend to the local community garden, learning about sustainable agriculture, patience, and the joy of growing your own food to share with others.

Growth Mindset Philosophy

King believes that "hard" just means "I haven't learned it yet." He embraces challenges as opportunities and understands that failure is a crucial part of the learning process. His motto: "Be 1% better every day."
