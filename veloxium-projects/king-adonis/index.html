Explore My Journey

Hi, I'm King Adonis!

I'm 8 years old and I believe that every day is a chance to build my own kingdom. Not a kingdom with castles and dragons (though that would be cool!), but a kingdom of character. It's built with the bricks of discipline from my jiu-jitsu training, the explorations from coding and reading, and the happiness I feel when I help others. This website is where I share my journey, the things I'm learning, and the adventures I'm having. I hope it inspires other young kings and queens to build their own kingdoms, too!

My Core Strengths

Discipline

From waking up early for practice to finishing my homework, I know that doing the hard work now makes me stronger for tomorrow.

Curiosity

I love asking "why?" and "how does that work?". Taking things apart (and sometimes putting them back together) helps me understand the world.

Resilience

I don't always win or get things right the first time. I've learned that falling down is part of learning to stand taller.

Kindness

Being strong isn't just about muscles. It's about being strong enough to be kind, share, and help others when they need it.
